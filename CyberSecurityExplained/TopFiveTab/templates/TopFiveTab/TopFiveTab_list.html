The Top Five


This page goes over the top five cybersecurity attack vectors, which include:

  • Phishing
    • -Fraudulent messages used to solicit information or compromise an organization.
  • Malware
    • -Software used to compromise a computer system or cause damage and disruption.
  • Insider Threats
    • -Malicious intent or activity from someone inside an organization with access to private information.
  • DDOS
    • -DDOS or Distributed Denial of Service attacks which focus on extreme disruption of network access.
  • IOT/SQL
    • -SQL is a type of code used for data management in applications, but can also be directed against applications. IOT or Internet of Things, include the multitude of devices with access or connections to data or network communications.

Just one of these attacks can be devastating, but are often used in combination drastically increasing the damage done to a target. Click on the icon above to navigate through each one.
